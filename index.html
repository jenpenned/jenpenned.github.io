Hello world!

I'm a data journalist who reports, writes and scours documents and spreadsheets for information. I also analyze and visualize data and make graphics. Although I gravitate towards stories about science, health and the environment, I get excited about all kinds of investigative reporting.

Outside of work, I doodle a lot. I like to carry at least one sketchbook with me for observational purposes. In my spare time, you can find me exploring my surroundings through urban sketching.

Urban sketching is about experiencing a place as you draw it, so I tend to pack light:



I use a Fude nib pen (the green one) for big bold lines and a finer nib for details. For color, I have a 12 half-pan palette of watercolors, with an added squeeze of sap green and ultramarine for the mountain and ocean views nearby. And a color-changing magic crayon because it makes me happy.

Did I mention I like sap green!
